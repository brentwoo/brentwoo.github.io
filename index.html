Hi, I'm Brent.

I am a linguist working in voice assistant technology. One of my missions is to bring the work of linguists more into public awareness. To that end, I once gave a TEDx talk on language technology. I'm also the only person who has worked at both the Linguist List and the Linguistic Society of America. I enjoy playing incredibly loud keyboard instruments and reading sad books.

I have a PhD in Linguistics at UW in Seattle, where I worked on coordination in English.
